Mudra Locker

Last updated: Jun, 2021

Introduction

Mudra Locker is the most affordable, secure and intuitive liquidity locker for BSC / BEP-20 tokens. It can be used to easily lock liquidity created on PancakeSwap.

Connecting Wallet

Wallets like Metamask will be detected and connected to automatically. For other wallets, please click on "Connect Wallet" button.

Once connected, it will show the connected account. In case of problems, please refresh the page.

Lock

You can lock your liquidity on PancakeSwap for BSC/ BEP-20 tokens easily using Mudra Locker. Please ensure that your wallet is connected (see previous section)


Step 1: Click on the "Lock LP Tokens" tab.


Step 2: Enter the liquidity token (LP token) contract address. If you have difficulty finding this, please use Mudra Research tool which provides links to LP tokens using your original token address (See Liquidity Pool Value metric).




Step 3: The tool will fetch the LP token balance in the current account. By default the available balance will be set for locking, you can enter a different amount in the amount input box.


Step 4: Select the unlock time using calendar or quick buttons "Tomorrow"/"+1 Year".


Step 5: Select the fee type. If you choose LP option, 0.5% of your LP tokens will be charged as fee. If you choose BNB option, a flat fee of 0.1 BNB will be charged. These are the lowest fees in the market, making Mudra Locker the most affordable solution for locking liquidity.


Step 6: Click on "Approve LP Token" button, and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.


Step 7: The approval may take few mins, once approved, click on "Lock LP Token" button, and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.


Step 8: Once your lock is created you can go to "Manage LP Token Locks" tab to manage your locks (see next section).

Manage Locks

You can withdraw, increase, extend and transfer your locked liquidity in an amazingly simple way with Mudra Locker




Step 1: To start managing your locks, go to "Manage LP Token Locks" tab.


Step 2: You will see a list of locks, click on a lock to view the manage options.

Unlock

You can unlock and withdraw your LP tokens once the unlock time is reached. There is no fee for unlocking tokens.


Step 1: Click on the "Withdraw Amount" Button.


Step 2: It will show the token amount that is eligible for withdrawal.


Step 3: Click on "Max" for withdrawing all tokens or enter a custom amount for partial withdrawal.


Step 3: Click on "Withdraw" button, and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.

Extend Lock Time

You can extend your lock period by setting a new time.There is no fee for extending lock period.


Step 1: Click on the "Extend Lock Time" Button.


Step 2: Enter the new time using calendar view or quick buttons. This time should be greater than current unlock time and current time.


Step 3: Click on "Extend" button and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.

Increase Lock Amount

You can increase the amount of LP tokens locked.


Step 1: Click on the "Increase Amount" Button.


Step 2: The tool will fetch available LP token balance in your connected account. Click on "Max" to lock all available balance or enter a custom amount.


Step 3: Select the fee type. If you choose LP option, 0.5% of your LP tokens will be charged as fee. If you choose BNB option, a flat fee of 0.1 BNB will be charged. These are the lowest fees in the market, making Mudra Locker the most affordable solution for locking liquidity.


Step 4: Click on increase and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.

Transfer Ownership.

You can transfer the lock ownership. The new owner will be able to manage the lock. You will no longer be able to manage it.


Step 1: Click on the "Transfer Ownserhip" Button. There is no fee for transferring ownership.


Step 2: Enter new owner address.


Step 3: Click on "Transfer" button, and confirm the transaction. Please note depending on your wallet the confirmation screen might look different.

Lock Certificate.

You can generate shareable lock certificate. This certificate can be shared on your website or in marketing posts as a proof of locked liquidity. There is no fee for generating certificates.


Step 1: Click on the "Generate Public Link" Button.


Step 2: Click on Shareable Link.


Step 3: This will open the lock certificate in new tab. You can copy and share the link as highlighted below.

Browse Locks

You can browse through all the tokens that are currently locked with Mudra Locker.

Step 1: Click on the "Browse All Locks" Tab.


Step 2: It will load all the locks. Since the information is being read from blockchain, this might take some time.


Step 3: On clicking on the lock, you will be able to see the lock certificate in a new tab.

FAQs

Is Mudra LP Locker secure?

Absolutely, 100+ projects are using the Mudra LP locker, you can browse through all the current locks.

What is the advantage of Mudra LP Locker?

Mudra Locker is the most affordable solution in the market. It has the most intuitive user interface to create and your manage lock. It is very secure and has no hidden agenda like promoting a utility token that many other lock providers have.

Is there a way to withdraw liquidity before unlock time?

There is absolutely no way to withdraw liquidity before unlock time from Mudra Locker.

What will happen when a new version of PancakeSwap is released?

Mudra Locker supports migration to a new version of PancakeSwap.

What is the recommended lock period?

Mudra Locker recommends at least an year and ideally a three- or five-year lock period to provide necessary confidence to the investors.

What is the recommended lock amount?

Mudra Locker recommends at least 80% of liquidity is locked.

Why liquidity should be locked at all?

Locking liquidity provides the confidence to the investors that the token developers will not withdraw and run away with the liquidity money. This is now a standard practice that all token developers follow.
